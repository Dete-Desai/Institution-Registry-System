Institution Registration Services

Services we provide

We provide registry of hospitals and learning institutions and keep track of records of the institutions and the related personel related to the institutions.

Health Institutions Registration

Health Institution are registered with the standard health and measurement policy of the health ministry of Kenya and records of their details are stored for analysis use.

Learning Institutions Registration

Learning Institution are registered with the standard education and measurement policy of the education ministry of Kenya and records of their details are stored for analysis use.

Personel Tracking

We track and keep records of personel related to each of the institutions. This means that records of patients and staff related to health institution and students and staff related to learning institutions are kept and provided for the user.
